Erica Nicole Hall - Engineering Portfolio

Recent graduate of the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB), where I studied biomedical and mechanical engineering as well as chemistry, resulting in a Bachelor's of Science in Biomedical Engineering awarded in May 2025. I also earned the distinctions of Magna cum Laude and distinguished honors through the Science and Technology Honors Program. During my undergraduate studies, I worked in the Tissue and Organ Chip Bioengineering Laboratory investigating the role of pneumonia infection in major adverse cardiac events in patients months to years after hospitalization. Most recently, I interned for the Department of Defense as a Mechanical Engineer.

This website showcases my projects both in and outside of my coursework and what I have learned from these experiences. Please see the "get in touch" section for the most recent copy of my resume and my contact information.
